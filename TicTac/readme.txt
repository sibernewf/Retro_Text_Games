📘 Player Documentation – TICTAC: Tic-Tac-Toe vs. the PDP
🎮 How to Play

You play tic-tac-toe against the computer (the “PDP”).

The board is a 3×3 grid, with positions numbered:

1,1   1,2   1,3
2,1   2,2   2,3
3,1   3,2   3,3


Enter your move as row,column. Example:

2,3

📋 Symbols

YOU = *

COMPUTER (PDP) = P

🕹️ Rules

You and the PDP alternate turns.

First to line up three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.

If the board fills without a winner, it’s a TIE GAME.

Illegal moves (already occupied squares) are rejected — you must try again.

📊 Example Game
YOUR MOVE ? 2,3
YOU  *  
* PDP *
PDP  *  

🏆 Objective

Beat the PDP if you can!

If both sides play perfectly, expect a tie.

▶️ To Play

Double-click run.bat in the game’s folder.

This will build and run the game, and keep the window open when it finishes.
